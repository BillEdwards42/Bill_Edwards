AI model training 深度學習模型訓練

For the finals of a graduate level class, I trained a deep learning model on the past 10 years of S&P 500 related information to predict the price.

It was quite an experience. I really learned a lot of machine/deep learning principles and data curation from it. 在一堂碩士課程的期末專案中，我使用S&P500過去十年的資料訓練了一個股價預測的深度學習模型。

我在這個過程中學到很多機械/深度學習的知識，也獲得了更多爬蟲、API使用和整理資料的經驗.

./assets/images/Cheat_dl.png
This is the first test result of our model, using a more unrealistic approach to testing. The results showed good trend prediction, the actual value of predictions aren't far off from the actual values.
這是我們模型的第一個測試結果，使用了較不實際的測試方法。測試結果顯示了非常好的趨勢預測，每一個測試點和實際值均相差不遠.
./assets/images/No_cheat_dl.png
This is the second test result of our model, using a more realistic approach to testing. The results showed bad prediction, the predicted trend are off compared to the actual value.
這是我們模型的第二個測試結果，使用了較實際的測試方法。測試結果顯示模型預測效果極差，預測走勢和實際值相差甚遠.
./assets/images/meme.png
Our conclusion is that deep learning is not a good fit for long term stock price prediction. The event-driven and macro-sensitive features of stock price makes it hard for neural networks to map out the future based on patterns in the past.
我們的結論是深度學習不適合長期股價預測. 股價事件導向和總經敏感的特性使神經網絡很難根據過去資料的模式來準確預測未來.

Internal Carbon Pricing research 內部碳定價研究

For the first half of 2024, I've been cooperating with my department chairman to come up with an Internal Carbon Pricing system to try to reduce our school's carbon emission with nothing but information and non-monetary incentives. I've studied successful cases aboard and have learned a great deal. Though the National Science and Technology Council ultimately did not back my research so it never got pass the planning stage, I got the sustainable investment research scholarship from Amundi, Europe's largest asset management firm, and they only hand this scholarship out to MIT and a few other schools! 在 2024 年上半年，我和我們的系主任合作，嘗試制定一套校內碳定價機制，想透過資訊與動機設計來減少學校的碳排放。為了此研究，我閱讀了許多國外的成功案例，收穫頗豐。雖然國科會最後並沒有支持我的研究，因此計劃始終停留在規劃階段，但我獲得了歐洲最大資產管理公司 Amundi 頒發的永續投資研究獎學金，而這項獎學金只有麻省理工學院 (MIT) 和少數幾所大學才有資格申請！

Taipei exchange compitition's finalist 櫃買中心競賽進入最終名單

This competition requires the attendees to choose financial products, analyze them, and compile a portfolio to compete. Not only did I utilized my financail knowledge for the products in the portfolio, for the allocation of it, I used Python to conduct a Monte Carlo analysis, which calculates the optimal allocation based on past price information. I learned about how to incorporate quatitative approach into finance and was able to utilize it in practice. 這場比賽要求參賽者挑選金融產品、進行分析並組合成投資組合來和彼此競爭。除了在投資組合中運用金融知識外，我還使用了量化分析，根據商品過去的價格資訊來計算投資組合的最適配置。透過這次的比賽經驗我學到如何將量化方法融入金融，並能實際加以應用.

這是我在這場比賽中獲得的證書。整體來說，這是一個很有趣的體驗。它給了我一個去思考如何將以資料為基礎的決策融入實際投資的機會，我也在這次經驗中獲益匪淺.

這是蒙地卡羅模擬的結果。我們針對先前選定的股票進行了一百萬種隨機配置，並計算了夏普比率（報酬／風險），找出在這一百萬種配置中能產生最高夏普比率的配置.
